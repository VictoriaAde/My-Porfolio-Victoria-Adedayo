About Me

Victoria is a Javascript Developer. She's been in tech for about a year and she has been able to build fully fledged and responsive websites with HTML and CSS.

She has also been really dedicated to aquiring more skills so she can do more for the world at large. She is very passionate about bringing as many women and girls into tech starting from ladies in her community, she believes in giving back to the community in any way she can.

Over the last two months, she has learnt about other aspects of tech but has come to realise that she is more passionate about building applications that solve problems and make life easy for others.

Victoria's hunger for knowlegde and determination caught the eye of a startup that made them to offer her an internship opportunity.

During the period of the internship which was for 3 months, she built the official websites which is now live. She also was part a part of a few live projects during her internship. It was a dream come through for her.
